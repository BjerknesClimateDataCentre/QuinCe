This is the setup for the ExternalsStandardsBean.getAffectedDATASETSTest in QuinCe. I've built this as a timeline that will be screenshotted and saved as an image in the repository.

The CALIBRATIONS and DATASETS lines show the initial positions of the calibrations and datasets. These are lableed with upper and lower case letters respectively.
INSERTION POINTS shows where either new calibrations will be created, or where calibrations will be moved to when having their dates changed.

The tests and their expected outcomes are in the spreadsheet CalibrationBeenGetAffectedDatasets.ods
